Sydney

Sydney is one of the finest city in the world. Do you want to go to New York for the big city experience, but also want to visit a exotic places with beautiful beaches? Sydney is the place to go! It has it all!

for more photos click here

Sydney is such an exciting city with so much to do! The Sydney Opera House and Harbour Bridge are must-sees, and the views around Circular Quay are amazing. Bondi Beach is perfect if you like surfing or just chilling in the sun, and you can even walk the Bondi to Coogee coastal trail for awesome ocean views.

The city is also full of cool food spots, from fish and chips by the water to trendy cafes. If you want a break from the busy streets, check out the Royal Botanic Gardens—they’re super peaceful and right next to the harbour.
