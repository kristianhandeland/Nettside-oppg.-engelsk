New South Wales - Tourist guide

Practical Travel Tips

If you’re heading to New South Wales, make sure you pack for all kinds of weather because it can go from sunny to rainy pretty quick. Public transport in Sydney is super easy to use, so grab an Opal card to get around on trains, buses, and ferries. Don’t forget sunscreen—even in winter the sun is really strong. If you’re planning to see famous spots like the Sydney Opera House or Bondi Beach, go early in the morning to avoid big crowds.
