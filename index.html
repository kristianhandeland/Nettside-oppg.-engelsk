Find the info you need when visiting the amazing state of New South Wales!

Hey there, Traveler! Get ready to explore New South Wales, one of the best parts of Australia! This place is like a giant playground with everything: a super-cool city, mega-beaches for surfing, huge exiting mountains, and the most dangerous spiders in the world, but don’t worry, they are very shy so you probably won’t see them.

New South Wales has the famous city of Sydney, wild nature parks where you can spot kangaroos and koalas, and lots of places to try yummy food. Whether you love shopping, surfing, hiking, or just chilling out, New South Wales has an epic adventure waiting just for you. Let's dive in!
