Newcastle

If you travel about two hours up the road from Sydney, you get to Newcastle! It is like a mini version of Syndey, but much more chill were you get more of a “non-tourist” vibe.

Here in New Castle, you kind of get the vibe that you’re a local, and there is amazing beaches and piknik spots but without all the city and tourism rush that it is in Sydney.

If you really want a good summer experience, you can drive up to Nelson Bay for some of the best beaches in the whole world!
